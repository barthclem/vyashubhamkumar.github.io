Why Choose Us ?

In India, every year, lakhs of professional graduates come out from colleges/ universities ready to get employed but their hopes get drenched when companies find them unfit for their requisite roles. Though, candidates spend time and money in learning different courses but at the knick of the interview moment, candidates realize that the learned courses didn't provide them the impetus to clear the interview.

Some of the companies have started the trend of hiring and training the candidates for their requirements but the majority of them still hunt for trained professionals. A shortage of skilled professionals is one of the main reasons for unemployment though plenty of opportunities are available in every sector.

To make candidates employable and helps companies find on job trained candidates Questease solutions in academic partnership with HPE and TIMTS (Times institute of management and Technical Studies) are offering 100 % Industry oriented courses.
